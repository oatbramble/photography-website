About Me!


Hi, I'm Anna! I am an Ottawa-based photographer interested in a variety of different photography styles. My passion for photography started when I took a photography class in high school. I bought my first camera (a Nikon D3400 ) shortly after. I mostly take photos as a hobby, and I occasionally post them on my Instagram (@bramblephotography_). However, I will take a headshot photo for you, and I would be happy to take photos of your pets (details on my Services and Portfolio page)! I have taken photos of dogs, cats, hamsters, and horses (horses and hamsters not pictured at the moment but I will add them when I find where I stored the pictures). Recently, I have upgraded my camera to a Sony A6700 . I hope to update this website semi-frequently with new photos I have taken! Thanks for stopping by :)
